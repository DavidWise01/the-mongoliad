# THE MONGOLIAD · MGL

> A sphere of **UD0** — the ROOT0 universe / biosphere. Domain: **BIBLÍON**.

The Mongoliad (MGL) — Neal Stephenson, 2010–2013. A secret order of warrior-monks resists the Mongol Empire in the wake of the 1241 invasion of Europe — conceived as an experiment in collaborative, app-delivered serialized fiction grounded in real medieval martial arts. THE BIG IDEA: An experiment as much as a novel: can a band of authors serialize a saga through an app, the way a band of monks plots to behead an empire? 8 emergents (the people + the ideas/tech), full bibliography, cross-linked into the shared Stephensonverse (Enoch Root + the Waterhouse/Shaftoe/Forthrast lineages). Dual-agent web-verified. One repo per book/idea, as ROOT0 asked.
